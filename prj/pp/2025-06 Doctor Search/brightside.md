# Brightside Health - Comprehensive Research Summary

## Overview
Brightside Health is an online mental health platform specializing in depression and anxiety treatment, offering therapy, psychiatry, and medication management through a subscription-based model.

## Insurance Coverage with Cigna

### Direct Partnership
- **Full Coverage**: "With Cigna, all of your care will be covered, including consultations, unlimited messaging, treatment monitoring and prescription management" - [Brightside Insurance Coverage](https://www.brightside.com/insurance-cigna/)

#### https://www.brightside.com/insurance-cigna/
Brightside's partnership with Cigna covers over 150 million lives nationwide and may cover all or part of their mental health care services. The platform offers appointments available in as little as 2 days, with 92% of patients meeting their clinician within 5 days. Services include care for mild to severe depression, anxiety, and other mental health conditions through personalized treatment plans. Patients receive 1:1 support from dedicated providers via virtual care with video sessions and anytime messaging, plus proactive progress tracking. Specialized programs include suicide prevention services.

## Key Recommendations

### Best For:
- Depression and anxiety treatment
- Users needing medication management
- Cigna insurance holders seeking cost-effective care
- Those preferring integrated therapy + psychiatry approach

### Consider Alternatives If:
- You have complex mental health needs beyond depression/anxiety
- You've experienced billing issues with subscription services
- You prefer therapy-only approaches without medication focus
- You need extensive customer service support

## Verification Steps

1. **Contact Cigna directly** to confirm your specific Open Access Plus coverage
2. **Check Brightside's insurance verification** during signup process
3. **Review your plan's mental health benefits** for copay amounts
4. **Consider starting with therapy package** before adding medication management
