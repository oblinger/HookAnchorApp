## Mental Health Therapy Coverage

### Eligibility Requirements
- **Primary Care Provider (PCP)**: Recommended but not required for Open Access Plus
- **Referrals**: PPO plans typically don't require referrals for outpatient mental health services
- **Prior Authorization**: May be required for hospitalizations and some outpatient care
- **Coverage Verification**: Contact Cigna directly to confirm behavioral/mental health service coverage

### Session Limits
- **Annual Limits**: Most Cigna Open Access Plus plans have **no strict annual session limits** for therapy
- **ACA Compliance**: No lifetime or annual dollar limits on essential health benefits
- **Plan Variability**: Specific limits vary by employer plan design - check your plan documents

### Costs & Coverage
- **Therapy Session Cost**: $2.50 - $77 per session (after deductible)
- **Copay Range**: $15 - $50 per session (typical)
- **Out-of-Pocket Range**: $80 - $200 per session (if deductible not met or no coverage)
- **In-Network vs Out-of-Network**: 
  - In-Network: Copay/deductible applies
  - Out-of-Network: Pay full fee upfront, may get partial reimbursement
- **Coverage**: Nearly all Cigna plans cover therapy, but extent varies by specific plan
