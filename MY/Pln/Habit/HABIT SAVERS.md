
### - SAVERS
The Miracle Morning
- **Silence** – Meditation or prayer to start the day calmly.
- **Affirmations** – Positive self-talk to reinforce goals and values.
- **Visualization** – Mentally rehearsing success and desired outcomes.
- **Exercise** – Physical movement to boost energy and health.
- **Reading** – Consuming motivational or educational material.
- **Scribing** – Journaling to clarify thoughts, reflect, and plan. [[Daily]]
