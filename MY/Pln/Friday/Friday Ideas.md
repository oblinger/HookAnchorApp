# Friday Night Date Ideas around Bernal Heights (organized taxonomy)

> **Scope** – The ideas below are all within San Francisco and reasonably close to Bernal Heights. They are organized by category to make it easy to browse different types of romantic outings.

## Dining & Drinks

### Casual & Comfort Food

- **Blue Plate (American) –** Cozy back‑patio and comfort classics like meatloaf and smoked‑tomato risotto[bernalheights.com](https://bernalheights.com/blog/where-to-go-for-a-romantic-date-night-in-bernal-heights/#:~:text=Blue%20Plate).
    
- **Angie’s Pizza (Pizzeria) –** Wood‑fired pies with creative toppings (e.g., goat cheese & dates) and a vinyl‑record atmosphere[theinfatuation.com](https://www.theinfatuation.com/san-francisco/guides/best-date-night-restaurants-in-san-francisco#:~:text=,pizzas%20and%20wine%20at%20Angie%27s).
    
- **Front Porch (Southern) –** Fried chicken, jerk pork and shrimp‑&‑grits served in a relaxed setting[bernalheights.com](https://bernalheights.com/blog/where-to-go-for-a-romantic-date-night-in-bernal-heights/#:~:text=The%20Front%20Porch).
    
- **El Buen Comer (Mexican) –** Homey restaurant with tamales, guisados and enchiladas[bernalheights.com](https://bernalheights.com/blog/where-to-go-for-a-romantic-date-night-in-bernal-heights/#:~:text=El%20Buen%20Comer).
    
- **Piqueo’s (Peruvian) –** Shareable small plates like ceviche and lomo saltado[bernalheights.com](https://bernalheights.com/blog/where-to-go-for-a-romantic-date-night-in-bernal-heights/#:~:text=For%20a%20taste%20of%20Peru%2C,looking%20to%20try%20something%20unique).
    
- **Mitchell’s Ice Cream (Dessert) –** After‑dinner spot for cones, sundaes or vegan scoops; open until 11 p.m. daily[mitchellsicecream.com](https://mitchellsicecream.com/#:~:text=ICE%20CREAM).
    

### Upscale or Special‑Occasion

- **3rd Cousin (New American) –** À‑la‑carte and tasting menus feature uni crème brûlée with caviar and porcini‑black‑truffle ravioli[bernalheights.com](https://bernalheights.com/blog/where-to-go-for-a-romantic-date-night-in-bernal-heights/#:~:text=If%20you%27re%20looking%20for%20a,for%20a%20romantic%20evening%20out).
    
- **La Ciccia (Sardinian) –** Husband‑and‑wife team serve Sardinian seafood like spicy baby‑octopus stew and spaghetti with bottarga[sf.eater.com](https://sf.eater.com/maps/best-date-spots-bernal-heights-noe-valley-restaurants-bars-cafes#:~:text=Where%20to%20lock%20it%20down%3A,La%C2%A0Ciccia).
    
- **Saru Sushi Bar (Sushi) –** Tiny sushi bar known for fatty‑tuna and yellowtail‑belly nigiri and specialty rolls[theinfatuation.com](https://www.theinfatuation.com/san-francisco/guides/best-date-night-restaurants-in-san-francisco#:~:text=On%20date%20nights%20when%20you,seared%20escarole%20and%20garlic%20ponzu).
    
- **Penny Roma (Italian) –** Covered courtyard with hanging lights serves seasonal pastas; split a bottle of natural wine[theinfatuation.com](https://www.theinfatuation.com/san-francisco/guides/best-date-night-restaurants-in-san-francisco#:~:text=The%20covered%20courtyard%20at%20Penny,and%20you%E2%80%99ll%20be%20all%20set).
    
- **Komaaj Mazze Wine Bar (Northern Iranian) –** Walk‑in bar serving mazze platters and pomegranate‑kissed dishes[theinfatuation.com](https://www.theinfatuation.com/san-francisco/guides/best-date-night-restaurants-in-san-francisco#:~:text=Komaaj%20Mazze%20Wine%20Bar).
    

### Drinking & Social Spots

- **Holy Water –** Neighborhood tavern with craft cocktails (After the Gold Rush, Zombie, Southern Exposure) and a private‑barrel bourbon selection[holywatersf.com](https://holywatersf.com/#:~:text=In%20the%20heart%20of%20Bernal,Heights%20Open%20daily%20at%203pm)[holywatersf.com](https://holywatersf.com/#:~:text=Open%20Daily%20at%203pm).
    
- **Arcana –** Natural‑wine bar and plant boutique with 20‑foot ceilings, lush greenery and live music; most events have no cover.
    

## Entertainment

- **Roxie Theater –** Historic Mission‑district cinema showing independent and classic films; fosters engagement with filmmakers and the community[roxie.com](https://roxie.com/about-us/mission/#:~:text=The%20Roxie%20Theater%2C%20a%20San,3%29%20nonprofit%20organization).
    
- **Make‑Out Room (optional)** – Dimly lit dance bar with themed DJ nights (’60s soul, ’80s hits); happy‑hour cocktails are inexpensive and the dance floor has disco lights[tripadvisor.com](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g60713-d3599269-Reviews-Make_Out_Room-San_Francisco_California.html#:~:text=Have%20been%20to%20SF%203,We%20loved%20it)[tripadvisor.com](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g60713-d3599269-Reviews-Make_Out_Room-San_Francisco_California.html#:~:text=This%20is%20a%20bar%20with,came%20in%2C%20but%20nobody%20danced).
    

## Outdoor & Scenic

- **Bernal Heights Park –** Hike to the summit for 360‑degree views of downtown San Francisco, the Golden Gate and Bay Bridges; there’s a stone labyrinth and picnic‑worthy grassy areas[weekendsherpa.com](https://www.weekendsherpa.com/stories/sunset-hike-at-bernal-heights-park-in-san-francisco/#:~:text=It%27s%20one%20of%20the%20best,park%20that%20beams%20at%20sunset).
    
- **Precita Park –** Large grassy lawn with shaded seating and picnic tables; dog‑friendly and great for afternoon picnics[bernalheights.com](https://bernalheights.com/blog/the-best-picnic-spots-in-bernal-heights-for-2025/#:~:text=Precita%20Park). Reservations for picnic tables are available and BBQ/alcohol are not allowed[sfrecpark.org](https://sfrecpark.org/993/Precita-Park-Picnic-Area#:~:text=Address).
    
- **Esmeralda Slide Park –** Hidden gem with two long concrete slides; it’s playful and fun even for adults[tripadvisor.com](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g60713-d10223775-Reviews-Esmeralda_Slide_Park-San_Francisco_California.html#:~:text=If%20you%20are%20walking%20to,your%20shoes%20to%20increase%20friction).
    
- **Mission Dolores Park –** 16‑acre park offering skyline views, palm‑shaded lawns, playgrounds and sports courts[travel.usnews.com](https://travel.usnews.com/San_Francisco_CA/Things_To_Do/Mission_Dolores_Park_66054/#:~:text=On%20the%20western%20edge%20of,Rhea%E2%80%99s%20Market%20%26%20Deli%20and). Open daily 6 a.m.‑10 p.m.[travel.usnews.com](https://travel.usnews.com/San_Francisco_CA/Things_To_Do/Mission_Dolores_Park_66054/#:~:text=Hours%20and%20Tickets).
