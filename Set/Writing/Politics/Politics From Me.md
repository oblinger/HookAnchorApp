

### 2024-09-10  Good People and Bad People

Liberals want to believe that in all important ways all humans are equal.
Thus a person in a bad situation is the result of bad circumstance.
Thus a key role of government is to help protect people from such bad situations.

Conservatives want to believe that in all important ways all humans make their own destiny.
Thus a person in a bad situation is the result of some badness in the person's character.
Thus a key role of government is to help protect good people from such bad people.
