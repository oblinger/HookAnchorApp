# Black\_Bean\_Soup --


    SAUTE
    - 2tbs     Veg Oil
    - 1med     Onion (chopped)  ~2/3C
    - 4cloves  Garlic (minced)
    - 1tbs     Cumin (ground)
    - .5tsp    Cayenne pepper (ground)
    ADD; SIMMER
    - 3cans    Black Beans (undrained)
    - 3C       Mild salsa
    - 1.5C     Chicken broth
