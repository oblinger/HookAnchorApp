# Habits.Veg\_Eating --


    === EAT VEGETARIAN-ISH ==

    GOAL:  Eat super healthy, with modest calorie reduction goals

    - HUNGRY -- Consciously trying to one eat only when hungry 
    - SNACKING -- Eat fruit, but mostly nothing.
      - Getting out of snacking habits in evening at home.
        . Avoiding *being* at home in evening
        . Don't have many snacks at home (not even the low cal shit)
        . DO allow any snack at any time it is really desired -- but really avoid triggerable patterns
    - MEDIFAST  -- Use medifast meals to fill gaps in the day.
        . Get setup to make the heated ones on the go
    - VEG HEAVY -- Eat more vegetarian.  allow grains, but less bread.
    - ANTI-TRIGGERS -- Avoid snacking or heavy eating based on time, or place triggers
