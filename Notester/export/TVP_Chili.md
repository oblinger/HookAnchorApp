# TVP\_Chili --

    CHILI

    CHOP FIRST
    BROWN
     1tbsp   Olive Oil
     1       Chopped Onions
    SAUTE
     2       Stalks celery, chopped
     2       Green bell peppers, chopped
     2       Jalapeno peppers, chopped
     3       Cloves garlic, chopped
     2       Bay leaves
     1tsp    Ground cumin
     2TBSP   Dried oregano
     1tsp    Salt
    ADD
     2x4oz   Can chopped green chile peppers, drained
     3C      TVP
     3x28oz  Cans whole peeled tomatoes, crushed
     1/4C    Chili powder
     1 TBSP  Ground black pepper
     15oz    Can kidney beans, drained
     15oz    Can garbanzo beans, drained
     15oz    Can black beans
     15oz    Can whole kernel corn   (Can Add near end)
    SIMMER 45 MIN
