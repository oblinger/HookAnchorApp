# Pepper\_Pods --

    ===  Fast And Easy Stuffed Peppers Ingredients  ===

    COOKER
    - 2C      Rice     (used 1C)
    BOIL      (3min)
    - 6       Green Peppers  (Good size) 
    FILL    
    - 1Can    Black Beans
    - 1C      Salsa
    ? grated chedder

    (up to Instructions for Fast And Easy Stuffed Peppers

    As you are cooking rice, have peppers in a pot of water and bring to a boil. 
    Boil peppers until they get soft. Before (about 3 min.) rice is done, add black beans & Salsa 
    When Peppers are soft, remove from pot and place on a dish. Fill the peppers with the rice mixture
    For even more flavor, Pour a little salsa on top before serving. Tastes great & perfect when you
