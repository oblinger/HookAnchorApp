# Medical\_Targets --



    TARGETS SPECIFIED BY CONSUMER REPORTS HEALTH SPECIAL ISSUE (2009)

    Blood Sugar
    - Fasting blood glucose <110mg/dl  (<100 is better)
    - Post-meal (2-hour) peak, glucose <140mg/dl
    - Hemoglobin A1c (HbA1c)            <7%
    Cholesterol
    - Total       < 200mg/dl
    - LDL         < 100mg/dl
    - HDL         > 40mg/dl (men)  >50mg/dl (women)
    - Triglycerid < 150gm/dl
    Blood Pressure  <130/80 mmHg
