# Nine\_thing\_successful\_people\_do\_differently --



    (1) BE SPECIFIC
    - Be specfic about your goal, such that you will know precisely when and if it is acheived.
    - CONTRAST -- between your goal and obstacles that separate you from those goals.
    - SPECIFIC -- be specfic about: the goals, the obstacles, and actions needed.
    - VISUALIZE -- Visualize achieving the moment of achieving the goal, and of the obstacles, and actions
    - WRITE -- Write a few sentences to make each of these clear

    (2) SIEZE THE MOMENT
    - PLAN -- Plan for times to take needed actions -- BE SPECIFIC
    - IF-THEN TRIGGERS -- Create if/then trigger to ensure critical actions

    (3) KNOW HOW FAR YOU HAVE TO GO
    - DISCREPANCY DRIVES MOTIVATION -- Clearly seeing discrepancy drives modtivation to fix
    - LESS -- Monitoring can be disruptive before task is familiar and understood
    - TO_GO vs TO_DATE THINKING -- too much to_date thinking can undermine motivation
    - END W. TO_DATE THINKING -- to highlight motivation.

    (4) BE A REALISTIC OPTOMIST
    - POSITIVE -- Think positively about the chances that you will succeed
    - *DO-NOT-UNDER-ESTIMATE-DIFFICULTY* -- disasterous for preparing for difficulty ahead.
    >> REALISTIC OPTOMIST -- Believes they will succeed -- but also believe they have to MAKE it happen
       thru thinks like: effort, careful planning, persistence, and choosing the right strategies.
    - VISUALIZE *STEPS* -- dont visualize success, visualize the STEPS you will take to have success.

    - IF/THEN -- consider if/then to remember prior successes & difficulties when doubts creep in
    - TO KEEP OPTOMISM REALISTIC -- visualize obstacles you will face and actions taken

    (5) FOCUS ON GETTING BETTER RATHER THAN BEING GOOD
    - BELIVE YOU CAN LEARN -- Believing you can GET the ability to achieve is as impt as beliving you can achieve goal
    - ALLOW SCREW UPS -- Give your self permission to screw up.  (you will screw up less)
    - GETTING-BETTER-GOAL -- Better goal: Get better rather than be good.
