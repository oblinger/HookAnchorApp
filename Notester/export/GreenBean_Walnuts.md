# GreenBean\_Walnuts --


    HEAT
    - 1tbs  Olive Oil
    ADD
    - 1tsp    Soy Sauce
    - .25tsp  Ground Ginger
    - .25tsp  Garlic Powder
    ADD & COAT
    - 1/2Cup  Walnuts halves
    BAKE 25min (Stir occasionally, till done)
    DRY on paper towel

    STEAM 10min till softened
    - 1Lbs    Trimmed fresh green beans
    REMOVE & TOSS
    - 2tsp    Fresh lemon juice
    - 1tsp    Olive Oil
    MIX Walnuts
