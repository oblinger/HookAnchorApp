# Old-Prj.Privacy --

    CONCERN 
    This system examines the content of email traffic, thus care must be 
    taken to ensure employee control over the confidentiality of its output. 
     
     
    OUR GUARANTEE 
    All outputs from this system will either be: 
    (1) Aggregate in nature (not specific to any individual's mail), or 
    (2) will be computed on an individual basis, and *only* reported to that 
        individual. 
     
    (See user interface)
