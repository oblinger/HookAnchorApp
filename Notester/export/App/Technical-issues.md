# App.Technical-issues --

    TECHNICAL ISSUES  (Why such a system might <<>> work) 
     
    1) Even a human could not determine the *relevant* expertise categories 
       given just mail data 
     
    2) Information is lost when mail is compressed to word counts 
     
    3) Privacy issues 
     
    4) Relevant infomation lost among irrelevant messages 
       - News group mail 
       - Pseudo spam mail (unsolicited mail) 
       - Forwarded email 
       - Orthogonal info in email (Lets go out for lunch today) 
       - Statistically correlated by causally unrelated information. 
         (The speech group's volley ball team) 
     
    ------ 
    What info is given on an inferred expertise? 
    - Strength & confidence of association? 
    - Degree of expertise?  novice, working-knoweldge, guru, etc.? 
     
    ------ 
    Benefits/Limitations 
     
    Benefits 
    - Identify primary area(s) of expertise 
    - Identify language skills 
    - Identify particular projects associated with person 
     
    Limitations 
    - Cannot determine areas of expertise that are currently inactive. 
    - Poor determination of quality of associated expertise. 
    - Experience with specific packages/applications (Word)
