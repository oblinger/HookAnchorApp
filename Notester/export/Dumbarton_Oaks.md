# Dumbarton\_Oaks --

    Location: Dumbarton Oaks is located at 1703 32nd Street, N.W., Washington, D.C. 20007 

    (one block east of Wisconsin Avenue between R and S Streets in Georgetown) 
    Garden Hours: Open daily (except Mondays). 
    15 March - 31 October: 2-6 pm, $7.00 admission; children and senior citizens $5.00. 
    1 - November-14 March: 2-5 pm, free admission. 
    Gates are locked at closing time. 
    Closed during inclement weather, federal holidays, and Christmas eve.
    Prices, open hours, and holiday closings are subject to change without notice.
    If you have questions about accessibility, please call the Office of the Director, 202-339-6410. 
    Garden Entrance: R and 31st Streets, N.W. 
    Garden season passes: $50 individual, $65 double, $75 family. 
    Please note that the museum and museum shop are now closed. We plan to reopen the galleries and museum shop in 2007, once the renovation of the main building is complete. The garden will remain open for the public. Please consult the Dumbarton Oaks website for updates.
    .
    Museum Entrance: 32nd Street NW between R and S Streets. 
    Transportation: Metrobus routes 30, 32, 34, 36, D2, D4, M12 come within two blocks of the garden entrance. On-street parking is available for up to two hours on weekdays and is unlimited on weekends. 
    Docent Tours: Guided tours for the museums and gardens are available and must be arranged in advance. Call 202-339-6409 for information or contact the Docent Coordinator: Christine Blazina <<>> e-mail: BlazinaC@doaks.org 
    For additional information: Write to Dumbarton Oaks, 1703 32nd Street, NW, Washington, D.C. 20007 <<>> call 202-339-6401 <<>> e-mail: DumbartonOaks@doaks.org

    Fax: 202-339-6419

    The facilities of Dumbarton Oaks are not available for the activities of outside groups or individuals.
