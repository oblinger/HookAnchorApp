# AAAIWS\_Dates --


    AAAI-05 WORKSHOP PROGRAM DEADLINES

    November 15
    AAAI sends packet of information to workshop organizers, including contracts, procedures, and preliminary production schedules by e-mail.

    November 22
    Workshop organizers submit via email the CFP's for their workshops to Adele Howe (howe@cs.colostate.edu), Peter Stone (pstone@cs.utexas.edu), and AAAI (workshops05@aaai.org) for inclusion in the Call for Participation, which will be posted on the AAAI web site in mid-December.

    Workshop contracts are due from organizers to AAAI by fax.

    March 11, 2005  (Now April 20)
    Recommended Workshop submission deadline

    April 15        (Now May 11)
    Recommended acceptance/rejection deadline, along with instructions on how and when to prepare the technical reports/working notes. Organizers send acceptance/rejection letters, A/V forms, and permission to distribute forms to participants (or information about where to find this information on the web).

    April 22        (Now May 13?)
    Organizers send AAAI lists of attendees (including name, affiliation, postal address and email) to workshops05@aaai.org.

    April 25 - May 6
    AAAI notifies workshop invitees of the location of registration materials on the AAAI web site.

    May 13
    * Organizer submits accepted paper list to workshops05@aaai.org.
    * Organizers advise AAAI whether they want their working notes to
      be part of the AAAI Technical Report series. This allows AAAI
      to distribute the volume after the symposium, and the work
      can be cited.
    * Organizers begin to prepare plain text of front matter, including
      title, chairs, organizing committee, and table of contents
      (order of papers).

    * Invited participants send A/V forms to workshops05@aaai.org.
    * Invited participants fax signed "Permission to Distribute"
      forms to 650-321-4457.
    * Invited participants submit electronic versions of abstracts
      and papers via the AAAI web site. Separate Publication
      Instructions will contain complete details.

    (For workshops with working notes only, authors should submit their papers directly to the organizer.)

    AAAI-05 Early registration deadline

    May 20   (Now May 18)
    Organizers send completed front matter to AAAI, including plain text versions of the following to workshops05@aaai.org:
     1) Title of Workshop
     2) Organizers names and affiliations
     3) Organizing or program committee names and affiliations
     4) Table of Contents (order of papers),
        if different from list submitted on May 13

    Organizers may also submit other camera-ready front matter for their working notes/technical report, such as a preface. These documents should be submitted through the AAAI web site. Instructions will be distributed in the spring.

    Organizers of workshops with working notes submit paginated camera-ready copy for printing and shipment by AAAI.

    June 10
    AAAI-05 Late registration deadline

    Organizers submit a PDF version of their schedule to workshops05@aaai.org for distribution at the workshop. These will be distributed to workshop attendees onsite.
