# Turkey\_Chili --


    SAUTE
    - 3tbsp   Olive Oil
    - 1Lg     Onion (minced)
    - 3cloves Garlic (minced)
    ADD+BROWN
    - 1Lbs    Turkey (ground)
    ADD&BOIL->SIMMER  (until thick; ~1hr)
    - 3C      Water                 50%
    - 28oz    Tomatoes (ground)     75%
    - 1small  Green pepper (minced)
    - .5tsp   Celery Seeds
    - .12tsp  Cayenne pepper
    - 1.5tsp  Cumin
    - 1small  Bay leaf
    - 2tbsp   Chili powder
    - .25tsp  Basil
    - 1.5tsp  Salt
    ADD&HEATcom
    - 15oz    Kidney beans
