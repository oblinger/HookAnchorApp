# life.54\_Tyranny\_Of\_Choice --


    Article Suggests:

    1) Choose when to choose
       - We can decide when to restrict our options when choice is not crucial.

    2) Learn to accept "Good enough"
       - Settle for a choice that meet your core requirements rather than searching for the elusive "best."  Then stop thinking about it

    3) Don't worry about what you are missing.
       - Consciously limit how much you ponder the seemingly attractive features of options you reject.
         Teach yourself to focus on the positive parts of the selection you do make.

    4) Control Expectations.
       - "Don't expect too much and you wont be disappointed" ius the cliche.
         But that advice is sensibleif you want to be more satisfied with life.
