# Camping\_Food --


    * roasted corn on the campfire (we may have some ears left by the weekend)
    * roasted veggie packets (chop a bunch of veggies in heavy duty foil and toss on the fire)
    * baked potatoes (wrap in foil, toss on fire, top with canned chili or whatever you like)
    * baked apples for dessert (wrap in foil)
    * grilled veggie dogs with buns and all the fixins
    * veggie burgers (premade or made from scratch)
    * wraps with hummus and grilled vegetables, for lunchtime on a hike
    * instant oatmeal with dried raisins or cranberries, nuts, chia seeds for breakfast
