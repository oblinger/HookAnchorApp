    --TIME TABLE--
    We use master schedule for our "big" events.  When convenient we build a schedule for a sub-project, but care
    must be taken to avoid missing dates buried in some sub schedule.  A schedule is a wiki table with the following fields:
    - S (Status):   '.'=Done, ' '=OnHold/Waiting,  '.'=WorkingOn,  '!'=Critical, '!!'=ReallyCritical, '!!!'=HolyCrap
    - Date:         Required completion date
    - Name:         Name of activity (typically a pointer to the project
    - Description:  Any text needed to clarify the task



    --PROJECT TREE--
    Projects and sub projects are put into a tree of wiki pages.  Subprojects can be embedded into their own pages
    or included in the parent as is convenient.  Format:

    - Name of wiki page is the project name (use '.' to denote subprojects as needed)
    - One sentence summary of project
    - Pointers to subprojects and related non-child pages too
    - Optional timeline (generally just use master)
    - Project Materials ...
