# 999forum.Conventions --

    The conventions for discourse are the rules that governing how the 
    XXXX proceeded.  These convensions include specifications of (1) the 
    mechanical procedures followed by the system, (2) specifications 
    regarding the display of the information, and (3) guidelines for 
    appropriate participant behavior. 
     
     
    Conventions are set for a particular node and are inherited  
    through the hierarchy. 
     
    > Conventions should be explicit, but should evolve over time. 
    > Conventions should be cover wide area (so no abuse) 
     
    > How to select and update convensions? 
     
    > Convensions accumulate (amend) down the tree, and can be added only 
      by a consensus at a node.
