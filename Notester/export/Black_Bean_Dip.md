# Black\_Bean\_Dip --


    MIX
    - 15oz    Black beans (canned)
    - 15oz    Corn (canned)
    - 1med    Red onion  (chopped)
    - .25C    Cilantro leaves (chopped)
    - 2       Tomatoes (whole/chopped)  OPTIONAL
    - 1       Green pepper (chopped)    OPTIONAL
    - 1pkg    Sausa mix or (wineVinegar, Fat free Italian Dressing, Garlic powder, tobasco sauce)  OPTIONAL
    ADD
    - 3tbsp   Olive oil
    - 1       Lime (juiced)
    - 1tsp    Cumin (ground)
    - 1       Jalapeno pepper (seeded)
    -         Salt/Pepper
