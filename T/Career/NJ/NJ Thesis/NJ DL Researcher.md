# Deep Learning Researcher Role Profile

## Key Experience Required

### Research Excellence
- PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field (or equivalent research experience)
- 3+ years of deep learning research with published papers at top-tier conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CVPR, ACL)
- Track record of novel contributions to deep learning theory or applications
- Experience with fundamental research in neural architectures, optimization, or learning theory
- Demonstrated ability to identify and solve open research problems

### Technical Depth
- Expert-level understanding of deep learning mathematics (backpropagation, optimization, information theory)
- Mastery of modern architectures (Transformers, Diffusion Models, CNNs, RNNs, GANs)
- Deep knowledge in specialized areas (NLP, Computer Vision, RL, Generative AI, Multimodal)
- Strong theoretical foundations in linear algebra, probability, statistics, and calculus
- Experience with neural architecture search and AutoML

### Implementation Skills
- Advanced proficiency in PyTorch and/or JAX (TensorFlow acceptable)
- Experience implementing papers from scratch and reproducing results
- Ability to write efficient, scalable research code
- Experience with distributed training and large-scale experiments
- Familiarity with CUDA programming and GPU optimization

### Domain Expertise
- Specialization in 1-2 deep learning domains (e.g., LLMs, Vision Transformers, Diffusion Models)
- Understanding of current research frontiers and open problems
- Knowledge of benchmark datasets and evaluation metrics
- Awareness of computational and theoretical limitations
- Experience with interdisciplinary applications

## Key Responsibilities

### Fundamental Research
- Propose and pursue novel research directions in deep learning
- Develop new neural architectures and training algorithms
- Advance theoretical understanding of deep learning phenomena
- Investigate emergent properties of large-scale models
- Push state-of-the-art on benchmark tasks

### Experimentation & Analysis
- Design and conduct rigorous experiments to test hypotheses
- Perform ablation studies and systematic analysis
- Scale experiments from proof-of-concept to large-scale validation
- Analyze failure modes and model behaviors
- Ensure reproducibility and statistical significance

### Publication & Dissemination
- Write high-quality research papers for top conferences
- Present research at conferences and workshops
- Contribute to research blogs and technical reports
- Peer review papers for major conferences
- Maintain open-source implementations of research

### Collaboration
- Work with interdisciplinary teams on applied research
- Mentor junior researchers and graduate students
- Collaborate with external academic researchers
- Partner with product teams to transfer research to applications
- Participate in research reading groups and seminars

### Innovation & Exploration
- Explore high-risk, high-reward research directions
- Investigate connections between different research areas
- Prototype novel applications of deep learning
- Challenge existing assumptions and paradigms
- Pioneer new research methodologies

### Technical Leadership
- Provide technical guidance on research directions
- Review and critique research proposals
- Establish research best practices and standards
- Lead research projects and initiatives
- Define long-term research vision

### Knowledge Transfer
- Bridge gap between research and production
- Translate research findings for engineering teams
- Create educational materials and tutorials
- Document research processes and findings
- Share expertise through internal tech talks

### Tool & Infrastructure Development
- Develop research tools and libraries
- Contribute to internal research platforms
- Optimize research workflows and pipelines
- Create reusable research components
- Improve research infrastructure efficiency

## Research Areas of Focus

### Core Deep Learning
- Neural architecture design
- Optimization algorithms and theory
- Generalization and learning theory
- Interpretability and explainability
- Efficient deep learning (pruning, quantization, distillation)

### Generative Models
- Large language models and scaling laws
- Diffusion models and flow-based models
- Multimodal generation (text-to-image, etc.)
- Controllable generation and editing
- Evaluation metrics for generative models

### Representation Learning
- Self-supervised learning methods
- Contrastive learning and metric learning
- Foundation models and transfer learning
- Few-shot and zero-shot learning
- Domain adaptation and generalization

### Advanced Topics
- Reinforcement learning and decision making
- Graph neural networks
- Neural differential equations
- Quantum machine learning
- Neuromorphic computing

## Success Metrics
- Publications at top-tier conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, etc.)
- Citation impact and h-index growth
- State-of-the-art results on benchmarks
- Patents and intellectual property
- Successful technology transfer to products
- Open-source project adoption
- Invited talks and keynotes
- Research grant funding (if applicable)
- Mentorship outcomes
- Internal research impact

## Technical Requirements
- **Languages**: Python (expert), C++ (beneficial)
- **Frameworks**: PyTorch, JAX, TensorFlow
- **Mathematics**: Linear algebra, calculus, probability, optimization
- **Tools**: Weights & Biases, TensorBoard, Jupyter
- **Computing**: SLURM, distributed training, cloud platforms
- **Version Control**: Git, experiment tracking systems

## Typical Background
- PhD in ML/AI or exceptional MS with research experience
- Previous roles: Graduate researcher, Postdoc, Research Scientist
- Publications at major ML conferences
- Experience at top research labs (Google Brain, DeepMind, FAIR, MSR)
- Strong mathematical and theoretical foundations
- Open-source contributions to major ML libraries

- Rigorous scientific methodology

- Ability to work on long-term uncertain projects
- Strong communication skills for complex ideas
