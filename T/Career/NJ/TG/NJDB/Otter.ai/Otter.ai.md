Meeting GenAI Chatbot – Otter’s generative AI assistant that can answer questions and generate content from past meetings. OtterPilot for Sales – a specialized application of Otter’s AI for sales call coaching. After calls, it auto-logs notes and insights into CRM, saving reps time and ensuring critical deal info is captured.
