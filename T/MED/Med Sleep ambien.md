## splitting it

I believe you're asking for a summary and URLs about splitting Ambien doses. Here's what I found:

  Summary of Ambien Splitting and Half-Dose Effects:

  Can You Split It?

  - Regular Ambien: Yes, can be cut safely
  - Ambien CR: Never split - destroys time-release mechanism

  Half-Dose Effects (5mg vs 10mg):

  - Not proportional: 5mg provides ~70-80% of 10mg's effect, not 50%
  - Duration: Minimal difference (both last 4-8 hours)
  - Individual variation: Some find 5mg fully effective, others need 10mg
  - Gender matters: Women typically need only 5mg, men 5-10mg

  Key Points:

  - FDA reduced standard dose from 10mg to 5mg due to next-day impairment
  - Many long-term users successfully use 5mg
  - Some people report no effect even at 10mg
  - Response varies dramatically between individuals
