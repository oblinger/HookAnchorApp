

## Example Tasking

Task: Understand and cure an alien disease.

**Given**:
- A measure of homostatic alien health from 0 to 100
- A healthy population generator
- A diseased population generator
- Mass Spectrometer with the ability to test samples from any named sub-part of the alien anatomy
- A list of available known biomolecules, along with their expected mass spectrometer readings

**Produce**:
- A treatment regimen that cures the disease within the afflicted population.
