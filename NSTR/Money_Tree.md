# Money\_Tree --


    * Non profit company that offers people high interest "Pay-day loans" which are used to build
      a financial cushion for the individual.


    * Customer gets an instant pay-day loan, and must pay it back next pay period with interest.
    * Each time surplus money is put towards the customer's balance.
    * INTEREST = CostOfMoney + ChargeOffInsurance + BuisnessExpense + Savings
    * Savings accrues with interest of a relatively low risk portfolio (~5%)
    * Over first 10-20 loans, charge off insurance drops to zero.


    RULES
    * Customer cannot withdraw this balance
    * Once balance reaches a certain size 


    PHASES
    BASE - First 10-20 one shot loans are each paid back at next pay period.
           At end of BASE period, no charge off insurance is needed to new one-shot loans.
    CUSHION - During phase increasing amounts saved into a cushion of 3or6 months of pay.
           Customer can borrow 1/2 cushion & pays interest back to self.
           Loss of job, or medical condition allows borrowing whole cushion (but not base)
    SAVINGS - once cushion is full extra monies can be pushed into investment accounts:
           IRA, College savings, 


    BACK OF ENVELOPE
    - Assume savings of 10% on $200 loans for $1000 <<>> job
    - 10 Loans for base (1 year)
    - Next 10 years  needed for 3 month cushion





    STATUS OF CUSTOMER
     - B = Balance of savings
     - L = Balance of outstanding loans.  Generally B>L except in the beginning.




    TERMS
    - Let 'C1' be the prob of charge off for first-time customers, C2 for second time, etc.
