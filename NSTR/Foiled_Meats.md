# Foiled\_Meats --


    BAKE @500 on lowest rack 15min

    MEAT 1.5Lbs  1/2 strips of chicken breast, pork tenderloin, center cut salmon, 
                 extra large shrimp, "dry" sea scallops
    SAUCE
    - .25C       Extra virgin olive oil
    - 4          Minced garlic cloves
      4          Thinly slices green onions (not italian)
    - 1tsp each  Salt, Ground pepper
    - LEMON DILL:
      .25C       Fresh dill
      1tsp.      Lemon zest
    - BARBEQUE
      1/2C       Barbeque Sauce
    - ITALIAN
      1C         Crushed canned tomatoes
      2tbs       Italian seasoning
      .25C       Capers

    VEG (1Lbs in 1" lengths)
    - Asparagus 
    - Bell peppers
    - Sliced Mushrooms
    - Bean sprouts
    - Sugar snap/snow peas
    - Cherry tomatoes
