    - Self-betrayal leads to self-deception and +ACI-the box.+ACI-
    - When you in the box, you can't focus on results.
    - Your influence and success will depend on being out of the box.
    - You get out of the box as you cease resisting other people.

    +AD4APgA+- STEP 2:  LIVING THE MATERIAL
    - Don't try to be perfect.  Do try to be better
    - Don't use the vocabulary--+ACI-the box,+ACI- as so on--with people who don't already know it.
      Do use the principles in your life.
    - Don't look for others' boxes.  Do look for your own.
    - Don't accuse others of being in the box.  Do try to stay out of the box yourself.
    - Don't give up on yourself when you discover you've been in the box.  Do keep trying.
    - Don't deny you've been in the box when you have been.

    - Don't focus on what others are doing wrong.  Do focus on what you can do right to help.
    - Don't worry whether others are helping you.  Do worry whether you are helping others.
