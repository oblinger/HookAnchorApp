# Rn.Rn Pi-Vs-Deductive --

    RN PI-VS-DEDUCTIVE: 
    Deductive logic paradigm:
    Each fact is a constraint on the world.
    World is constrained further by many facts since
    it must be in the intersection of the set of models that satisfy the 
    constraints.
    Plausible logics use their facts as a basis for construction of possible
    relationships between the predicates in the world.
