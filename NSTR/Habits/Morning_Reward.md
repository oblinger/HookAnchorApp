# Habits.Morning\_Reward --


    PROCESS
    - Add 2 hours to your first waking time, this is the "Reward Time."
    - Any commuting time gets added to the end of the reward time.
    - Once arriving at work location, timer is set end of the reward time.
    - Work is queued up (files opened, etc.)
    - Remaining time is used as desired.
    - Work on prime task begins for some specified period of time.





    OBJECTIVE:  Enjoy mornings, get moving quickly, and be effective in those hours.


    DETAILS:
    * WAKE -- this is the 


    WAKING TIME ALLOTMENTS
    * Wake    -- First time you look @ clock, and have had more than 5 hrs sleep
    * Snooze  -- 20 minutes
    * Dress   -- 30 minutes
    * Think   -- 30 minutes
    * Reward  -- 40 minutes
    * Commute -- 0-15-30 minutes
    * REWARD END 2.0-2.5 hours after waking
