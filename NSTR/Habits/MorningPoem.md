# Habits.MorningPoem --


       Waking up this morning I smile,
    Twenty-four brand new hours are before me.
       I vow to live fully in every moment,
    and to look at all beings with an eye of compassion.

    Smile;24;fully;compassion

     -- From the book   "Present Moment, Wonderful Moment"
        Plum Press,  Paralax Press, CA
