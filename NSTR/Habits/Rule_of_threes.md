# Habits.Rule\_of\_threes --



    <<>>


    1. 3 outcomes you want to achieve during the year
            This could be about growing the startup revenue by 3x, learning a new language and reducing 20 pounds. 

    2. 3 outcomes to achieve during the week
            Every Monday, do a quick check on what all things to be achieved in the week and condense it to 3 top things. This could be about launching the product, running 30km during the week and finish the detailed research report

    3. 3 outcomes for the day
            Every morning set 3 things to achieve for the day and do it as early as possible in the day. This could be like A) Finish feature x, B) Run a full test C) Migrate the server.
