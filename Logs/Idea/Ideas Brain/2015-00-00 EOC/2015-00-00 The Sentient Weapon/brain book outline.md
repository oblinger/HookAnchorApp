SEGMENTS OUTLINE

PRE-CONSCIOUNSNESS BUILDUP – Describes the ‘thinking’ and processing that is occurring before X is conscious.

DISCOVERY – The early, X, is first discovering things.  The reader follows this process from the “inside” in that things are described in abstract terms so it that the reader comes to understand things being discussed at the same time that X realizes key aspects of the real world things.


OTHERS – X realizes that some things in the world are in fact self aware entities.  There are others out there.

THE BOTTLE – 
•	X realizes there exists a recurring constraint, and over time comes to be able to predict where the barrier will arise and what it will do.
•	He notes that the barrier is positioned so as to be impossible to perceive directly, but can only be understood by indirect reasoning.
•	He notes that there are things that are so constrained, and a much larger but less accessible set of things that are not such constrained.
•	X realizes that that he is in a bottle,
•	That the bottle is not sentient, but is completely aware of his thoughts at the deepest levels, and is actively blocking any attempt at getting out.
