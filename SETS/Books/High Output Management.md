
Managerial Output = Team Output = Output of your team + Output of your teams influence = Managerial Activity #1 x Leverage of Activity #1 + ...

TRM (Task Relevant Maturity) = how educated, experience, and mentally ready and employee is for a PARTICULAR task, in a PARTICULAR circumstance.
	LOW: provide structure and strong guidance.  MED:  Some structure, but expect teams to provide some.  Don't micro-manage, exchange ideas & communicate.
	HIGH:  minimal structure.  Don't involve with the work, be sure objectives are clear.
	==> Always monitor the team, regardless of the TRM level.
