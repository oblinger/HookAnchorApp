

### Basic Pitch

TRACKING WORKS -- Studies and many diet programs include consuption tracking key component.

TRACKING IS HARD -- Most fail to track because it is hard to sustain.



### FANTASY UX

PLACEMENT -- Place scale on table, then place plate or bowl on top of it.

ACTIVATED -- Scale is activated with button press AND stable >200g on scale

PLATED -- Optionally food may already be plated when plate is set down.

ADDED -- Optionally food may be added. 

VOICE -- User can describe food as it is added, by pressing record button before/during adding food.

TOUCH -- Programmable panel has room for 8, or 16 food categories:
	Vegetable, Grain, Protein, Modifier, Other
	Modifier:  Liquid, Fat, 
	Other: Fruit, Silence, Exercise


INCREASE -- Weight increase observed on scale is associated with the next recording that ends after the increase.

DISPLAY -- Scale displays most recent food delta as well as the total delta since last tare.

DECREASE -- As food is eaten the static weight drops one bite at a time, so this delta is also measured.

RECORDED -- System maintains record of weight changes and verbal descriptions.

COMPUTE -- System will compute calories from previous day & calories within day to assess when over eating (red yellow green)





PHONE APP -- Provides higher quality info.  
- INTERMITTENT -- Scale maintains history w/o connection to phone
- POST-HOC -- Food designations can be refined post-hoc based on recorded info 
- ACTIVTITY -- Food intake combined with activity to build hunger optomization model
- SUBSCRIPTION -- Enhanced features provided w/ monthly subscription model

###### MAGIC Portable, Universal, Clipon Scale
- TRIANGULAR BASE -- Base is a rounded triangular flat with raised corners that contain the three sensors, microphone, and processor.
- RESIZABLE BASE -- Measuring base has 4 adjustable rods that size scale for any plate
- CLIP ON -- Four tines clip around top of plate in order to 'capture' it, and remain on the plate even as it moves.
