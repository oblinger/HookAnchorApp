## 2025-10-30  Getting Wegovy

Requirements
- BMI > 27  (28.8 ~ 5'9" 195lbs)
- High Cholesterol
- Cardiovascular Disease
- Fatty Liver

 866-727-5867


 Step 1: Call Express Scripts

  Phone: 866-727-5867

  Questions to ask:
  1. "Does my plan cover Wegovy under the EncircleRx Weight Loss Program?"
  2. "Does my plan have the $200/month cap option?"
  3. "Do I need prior authorization?"
  4. "How do I enroll in the Omada program?"
  5. "What documentation do I need from my doctor?"

  Step 2: Talk to Your Doctor

  You'll need:
  - Prescription for Wegovy
  - Documentation of your BMI (28.8)
  - Medical records showing:
    - High cholesterol (on 80mg Lipitor)
    - Cardiovascular disease diagnosis
  - Prior authorization form (if required)

  Step 3: Enroll in Omada Program

  - This is required for coverage starting March 1, 2025
  - Virtual health program (app-based)
  - Express Scripts will provide enrollment details

  Step 4: Submit Prior Authorization

  - Your doctor submits this with medical necessity documentation
  - Include BMI calculation and comorbidities
  - May take 1-2 weeks for approval

  Step 5: Fill Prescription

  - Once approved, fill at Express Scripts pharmacy
  - 30-day supply
  - Pay up to $200/month (if your plan has the cap)

  Start with the Express Scripts call - they'll walk you through the specific process for your plan. Want me to help you prepare any other questions?
