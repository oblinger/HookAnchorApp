  Animated Adventures

  1. Puss in Boots: The Last Wish (2022)

    - Adventure with fun characters

  2. The Mitchells vs. The Machines (2021)
    - Family vs. robots, super fun
    - Colorful, fast-paced
    - Netflix original
  3. Ron's Gone Wrong (2021)
    - Kid with malfunctioning robot friend
    - Heartwarming and funny
    - Great friendship story
  4. Strange World (2022)
    - Disney adventure with creatures

    - Beautiful visuals
  5. Raya and the Last Dragon (2021)
    - Quest to find dragons
    - Action-adventure

  6. Kubo and the Two Strings (2016)

  7. The Willoughbys (2020)

  1. Clifford the Big Red Dog (2021)
