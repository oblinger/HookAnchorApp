# Glossary

## Reports

### Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) / Environmental Impact Report (EIR)
Evaluates potential environmental effects of proposed construction. Assesses air quality, water resources, soil contamination, wildlife habitats, noise, etc. Required for most major construction projects.

### Phase I Environmental Site Assessment (ESA)
Identifies potential environmental contamination liabilities. Reviews historical site usage, hazardous materials, and contamination risks. Standard requirement before property acquisition or development.

### Geotechnical Report (Soils Report)
Analyzes soil composition, stability, and load-bearing capacity. Identifies groundwater levels, seismic risks, and foundation requirements. Prepared by a Geotechnical Engineer.
