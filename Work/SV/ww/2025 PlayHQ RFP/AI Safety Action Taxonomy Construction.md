# AI Safety Action Taxonomy Construction

## Relevant Papers

| Paper | Year | URL | Summary |
|-------|------|-----|---------|
| TaxoAdapt: Aligning LLM-Based Multidimensional Taxonomy Construction to Evolving Research Corpora | 2025 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.10737 | Dynamically adapts LLM-generated taxonomies through iterative hierarchical classification, expanding taxonomy width and depth based on corpus topical distribution |
| Self-supervised Topic Taxonomy Discovery in the Box Embedding Space | 2024 | https://direct.mit.edu/tacl/article/doi/10.1162/tacl_a_00712/125175 | Uses box embeddings (concepts as boxes, not points) with asymmetric metrics to infer hierarchical relations through recursive clustering |
| TaxoGen: Unsupervised Topic Taxonomy Construction by Adaptive Term Embedding and Clustering | 2018 | https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.09551 | Unsupervised method using adaptive term embeddings and recursive hierarchical clustering to construct topic taxonomies from text corpora |
| The Artificial Intelligence Ontology: LLM-Assisted Construction of AI Concept Hierarchies | 2024 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.03044 | Systematizes AI concepts and interrelations using Claude 3 Sonnet and GPT-4 with template-based ontology construction |
| LLMs4OL 2024: The 1st Large Language Models for Ontology Learning Challenge | 2024 | https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.10146 | Challenge with three tasks: Term Typing, Taxonomy Discovery (is-a hierarchies), and Non-Taxonomic Relation Extraction |
