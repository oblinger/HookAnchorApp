                         ***  WELCOME TO NOTESTER  ***

    Notester is a tool for organizing the information of your work, and life.

      **   Notester provides the simplicity of a simple time-ordered journal or diary.
      **   At the same time Notester lets you view that data by name as a collection
           of web pages organized and reorganized around each of your life's topics.
      **  It also presents that same information as todo lists which you use to 
          plan (and continuously replan) your day, your week, your year, etc.
