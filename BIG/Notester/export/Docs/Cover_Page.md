# Docs.Cover\_Page --



    Notester is a tool for organizing the information of your work, and life.

      (1) Notester provides the simplicity of a simple time-ordered journal or diary.
      (2) At the same time notester lets you view that data as a collection of web pages
          organized and reorganized around each of your life's topics.
      (3) It also presents that same information in as todo lists which you use to 
          plan (and continuously replan) your day, your week, your year, etc.


    Press the CTRL and "H" keys to view Notester's help tutorials.
