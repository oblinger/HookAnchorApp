# 1106-IRA-analysis --

    IRA

    - Suppose $100K invested
    - over a period where the markets go up by 10x.
    - Suppose a 30% tax bracket now, 30% or 40% at retirement, and 15% cap gains tax.


    SUMMARY
    $610  Taxable
    $700  Roth
    $700  IRA (no shift in taxes)
    $600  IRA (33% increase in taxes)


    TAXABLE ACCOUNT
    - 100K gross income in
    -  30K tax
    -  70K invested in one stock
      700K at retirement
       90K tax (15% of 600K)
      610K post tax at retirement 

    ROTH
    - 100K gross income in
    -  30K tax on 100K
    -  70K invested
      700K at retirement    <---- money @ retirement

    IRA    (NO SHIFT IN TAXES)
    - 100K gross income in
    - 1000K at retirement
    -  300K tax
    -  700K <----- money @ retirement

    IRA  (INCREASE IN TAXES)
    - 100K gross income in
    - 1000K at retirement
    -  400K tax
    -  600K <----- money @ retirement
