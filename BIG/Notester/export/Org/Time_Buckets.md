# Org.Time\_Buckets --



    TIME BUCKETS
    - FRITTER              -- 1 hr    Time spent between the gaps in things
    - ADMIN     (penny)    -- 1.5 hr  Reading email
    - MEETINGS  (dime)     -- 1 hr    Meetings, Tcons, ...
    - TASK      (nickel)   -- 1 hr    Important Products (including meetings)
    - QUAD2     (quarter)  -- 1 hr    Quad 2 Tasks

    QUAD TWO TASKS
    - Writing
    - Creation Project
    - Deep planning

    PROBLEM:   Details push impt stuff to the side.
    SOLUTION:  Ensure work done in critical buckets each day in addition to other work.
    APPROACH:  Use 90 min buckets.  Do 1-2 Task/Quad2 activities per day.
