    [MAIN IDEAS]

    INSIDE-OUT
    - Personality (secondary) verses Character (primary) Ethics 
    - Paradigm: matters, principle-centered,

    OVERVIEW
    - Habits:  Intersection of Knowledge (what to, why to), Skill (how to), Desire (want to)
    - Habits:  Internalized principles and patterns of behavior
    - Maturity Continuum:  Dependence, Independence, Interdependence

    #1 -- BE PROACTIVE
    - There is a gap between stimulus and response
    - Take the initiative: Act or Be Acted Upon
    - Response-able.  
    - Focus on energy on circle of influence, and it will grow toward your circle of concern.
    - Key is making and keeping commitments.
    - 30 day proactivity test.  Listen to your language.

    #2 -- BEGIN WITH THE END IN MIND
    - All things are created twice:  (Plan first; become your own first creator)
    - Center: Wisdom, Security, Guidance, Power
    - Alternate Centers: Family, Money, Work, Possessions, Pleasure, Friend/Enemy, Church
      My Alternates:  Status? Efficiency? Time?
    - Principled cenered
    - Write mission statment

    #3 -- PUT FIRST THINGS FIRST
    - Quadrand II: Important but not urgent
    - Delegation: 
      Mutual Clear Results.  
      Guidelines (boundaries). 
      Identify their resources: human, financial, technical, organizational.
      Accountability.  Define standards that will be used in evaluating later.
      Consequences.  Good and bad as result of evaluation.

    PARADIGMS OF INTERDEPENDENCE
    - Emotional Bank Account.  Six deposits:
      Understanding them.  
      Attend to the little things.  
      Keeping commitments.  
      Clarifying Expectations.  
      Showing Personal Integrity.  
      Apologize.

    #4 THINK WIN/WIN  (or no deal)
    Five Dimensions of win/win: char->relationships->agreements
      (1) Character:      Integrity (value placed on ourselves), Maturity (balance of courage&consideration), Abundancce Mentality
      (2) Relationships:  (symetric high-bank accounts)
      (3) Agreements:     Desired Results, Guidelines, Resources (human, fin, technical, org support), Accountablity, 
                          (natural) Consequences:  Financial,Psychic,Opportunity,Responsibility
      (4) System:         Rules that reward win/win
      (5) Processes:      (1) See Others View; (2) Identify Issues (not positions); (3) Determine fully acceptable results; (4) Identify new ways to achieve those results
      
    #5 SEEK FIRST TO UNDERSTAND, THEN TO BE UNDERSTOOD
