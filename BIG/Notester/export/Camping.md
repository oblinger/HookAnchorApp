    CAMPING: Things to take on a camping trip 
     
     
    SHELTER 
      Tent 
      Ground Cloth  
      Sleeping Bag, 
      Supporting Pad (pump)
     
    CLOTHING 
      Backpack  
      Poncho or other rain gear 
      Hiking Boots  
      Socks (preferrably wool + quick drying non-wool liners) 
      Windbreaker or sweatshirt 
      Shorts 
      Pants (light colored is recommended to see ticks and bugs) 
      Shirts (guide recommends wool everything but that's impractical) 
      Underwear 
      Handkerchiefs 
      Gloves, Hat, Sun Glasses
      Lightweight second pair of shoes 
      Swim suit 
      Coat 

    COOKING 
      Stove, Fuel, Lighter, Tongs, Spatula
      Pots, Cup (for eating out of) 
      Water bags/bottles, Iodine Tablets 
      Food,  Wine
      Disposable: Napkins, Plates, Silverware, Cups
