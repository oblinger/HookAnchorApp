# Habits.Boxed --

    - just try to block time this week

    [PERSONAL PROACTIVITY HABIT]
    - Plan day as a set of timed blocks.
    - At the beginning of *EACH* time block: 
      Decide a block of time is a PP, ST, PT, or DT session, record the time, 
      and choose expected end time.


    [PPS: PERSONAL PROACTIVITY SESSION]  
         --  These are moving me fwd in impt directions (freeform w. no deadlines)
    - <30 MIN STARTUP.  At start of PP session at most 30 minutes of misc 
      personal tasks before begining.
    - Select PP goal, and identify sub-goal(s) for this session

    [STS: SHORT TASK SESSION]   (Chew)
    - Order tasks, and add tasks to personal task list before starting

    [PTS: PRIME TASK SESSION]   (Work)
    - Jump into task for specfied amount of time, or
      until specified goals is achieved, or
      until interrupted

    [DTS: DEFINED TASK SESSION]   one of:
      PLAN  -- Planning screen
      WEEK  -- Weekly task list




    [BACKGROUND INFO]
    - I rarely work on most impt personal activities.
    - I don't plan what I will do
    - I don't have managable steps for most impt tasks
