### RULES

#### __  XXX ___             A Logical Drive
#### __ XXX                     Part of a logical drive
#### large                       Location on main laptop where Master copies of logical drives are stored
#### __ MASTERS __     Folder on Master USB where "Master" copies are stored
#### __ BK                        Location where undated backups of logical drives are stored


Where to store some data:
- _ _ BK 				is it a BACKUP of an ENTIRE logical drive?
- _ _ SNAPSHOT _ _	is it a BACKUP of some other kind?
- _ _ XXX  			is it a TRANSFER some of PART of the logical drive 'XXX'?
- _ _ ARCHIVE _ _		is it a TRANSFER of some part of the 'OB' folder tree?
- _ _ IMAGES _ _		is it a TRANSFER of an entire filesystem, image, etc.?
	- _ _ OFFLOADS _ _ 	is it ma TRANSFER of some other kind?






## Rules (2015)

* _FOLDERS_ in all caps, with underbars before and after are logical drives.
    * In the root directory of any drive means this is the master version of the logical drive
	* In the __BK directory in the root of any drive means it is a full copy of the logical drive
	* The only legal location for a logical drive is in the root folder of another logical drive
