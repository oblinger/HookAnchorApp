
The Edu Corp idea is elegant and general---unfortunately the reality of actual business are typically neither of these things!  This Edu Corp Tweaks document provides an open ended list of approaches one might take in accommodating a range of pragmatic issues that arise as one attempt of fit the reality of ACTUAL businesses into the theoretical EduCorp model.  In the cases listed there are ways to accomodate aspects of businesses that are breaking the Edu Corp's simplest model in a way that retains much or all of the assurances and benefits provided by the EduCorp model.  These "Tweaks" are captured here outside the basic model since the basic model is already complex, trying to accomodate ALL of these special cases with a single system would become unworkable.  Fortunately one can introduce these tweaks only as needed on a business by business basis, thereby keeping the complexity of the model used reasonable.
