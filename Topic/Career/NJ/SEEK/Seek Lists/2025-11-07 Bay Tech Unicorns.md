## Notes & Methodology

**Data Sources:**
- Public market cap data as of November 2025
- Silicon Valley Index 2024 report
- Company investor relations and SEC filings
- Financial data providers (Yahoo Finance, Google Finance, etc.)

**Definitions:**
- **Unicorn**: Private company valued at $1B+
- **Decacorn**: Private company valued at $10B+
- Market caps fluctuate daily with stock prices

**Geographic Scope:**
- San Francisco Bay Area includes: San Francisco, San Jose, Oakland, Palo Alto, Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Santa Clara, Fremont, and surrounding cities
- Silicon Valley specifically refers to the South Bay (Santa Clara Valley)
- Companies included if HQ or major operations in Bay Area

**Industry Classifications:**
- Focus on technology-first companies
- Includes tech-enabled companies across sectors
- Some companies span multiple categories

**Count Summary:**
- **Trillion+ companies**: 5
- **$100B+**: ~20
- **$10B-$100B**: ~50
- **$1B-$10B**: ~70+
- **Total public companies $1B+**: ~145+
- **Private unicorns**: 276
