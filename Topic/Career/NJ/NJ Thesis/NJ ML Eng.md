# Machine Learning Engineering Manager Role Profile

## Key Experience Required

### Technical Expertise
- 8+ years of hands-on ML engineering experience
- Strong programming skills in Python, with expertise in ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, JAX)
- Experience with distributed computing and large-scale data processing (Spark, Ray, Dask)
- Deep understanding of ML algorithms, model architectures, and optimization techniques
- Hands-on experience with MLOps tools and practices (MLflow, Kubeflow, W&B)

### Engineering Leadership
- 3+ years managing ML engineering teams (5-20 engineers)
- Experience hiring, mentoring, and developing ML engineers
- Track record of delivering ML systems from prototype to production
- Experience with agile development and project management in ML contexts
- Ability to balance technical depth with people management

### Production ML Systems
- Experience deploying and maintaining ML models at scale
- Expertise in model serving infrastructure (TorchServe, TensorFlow Serving, Triton)
- Knowledge of A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Experience with model monitoring, drift detection, and retraining pipelines
- Understanding of latency, throughput, and cost optimization for ML systems

### Data & Infrastructure
- Experience with data pipelines and feature engineering at scale
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI, Azure ML)
- Understanding of containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with GPU/TPU optimization and distributed training
- Familiarity with data versioning and experiment tracking

## Key Responsibilities

### Team Leadership
- Build and manage a team of 5-20 ML engineers
- Conduct hiring, performance reviews, and career development
- Foster collaborative culture between ML engineers and data scientists
- Set technical standards and best practices for the team
- Balance team velocity with technical excellence

### Technical Delivery
- Own end-to-end delivery of ML features and products
- Ensure models move efficiently from research to production
- Design scalable ML architectures and systems
- Review code, models, and system designs
- Troubleshoot complex ML system issues

### Project Management
- Partner with product managers to define ML roadmaps
- Estimate timelines and resource requirements for ML projects
- Manage multiple ML initiatives simultaneously
- Communicate progress and challenges to stakeholders
- Prioritize projects based on business impact and feasibility

### Infrastructure & Operations
- Design and implement ML infrastructure and platforms
- Establish MLOps practices and CI/CD pipelines
- Optimize model serving for latency and cost
- Implement monitoring and alerting for ML systems
- Ensure reliability and uptime of production models

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- Partner with data scientists on model development
- Work with data engineers on feature pipelines
- Collaborate with platform teams on infrastructure
- Coordinate with product teams on requirements
- Interface with business stakeholders on ML capabilities

### Model Lifecycle Management
- Oversee model training, validation, and deployment processes
- Implement model versioning and rollback strategies
- Establish model governance and documentation standards
- Monitor model performance and trigger retraining
- Manage technical debt in ML systems

### Innovation & Best Practices
- Stay current with ML engineering trends and tools
- Evaluate and adopt new technologies and frameworks
- Contribute to open-source ML projects
- Share knowledge through tech talks and documentation
- Drive continuous improvement in ML engineering practices

### Quality & Testing
- Establish testing strategies for ML systems
- Implement data quality checks and validation
- Design offline and online evaluation metrics
- Ensure reproducibility of ML experiments
- Maintain high code quality standards

## Success Metrics
- Model deployment velocity (time from development to production)
- System reliability (uptime, latency SLAs)
- Model performance metrics (accuracy, precision, recall)
- Team productivity and delivery metrics
- Infrastructure cost per prediction/training run
- Team retention and satisfaction scores
- Code quality metrics and test coverage
- Number of models in production
- Incident response and resolution time
- Stakeholder satisfaction ratings

## Technical Skills Required
- **Languages**: Python (expert), SQL, Scala/Java (preferred)
- **ML Frameworks**: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, XGBoost
- **MLOps Tools**: MLflow, Kubeflow, Airflow, DVC
- **Cloud Platforms**: AWS, GCP, or Azure ML services
- **Containers/Orchestration**: Docker, Kubernetes
- **Monitoring**: Prometheus, Grafana, custom ML monitoring
- **Version Control**: Git, GitHub/GitLab
- **Data Tools**: Spark, Pandas, Arrow, Parquet

## Typical Background
- BS/MS in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
- Previous roles: Senior ML Engineer, Tech Lead, Staff Engineer
- Industry experience in tech, finance, healthcare, or e-commerce
- Open source contributions or published papers (preferred)
- Experience with both research and production ML systems
