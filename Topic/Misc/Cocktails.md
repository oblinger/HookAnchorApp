### Old Fashion
2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
1 sugar cube (or 1/2 teaspoon simple syrup)
2-3 dashes Angostura bitters
Orange peel
Ice
Optional: cherry for garnish
Instructions:

Muddle the sugar cube (or simple syrup) with bitters and a few drops of water in a glass.
Add a large ice cube (or several smaller cubes), then pour in the whiskey.
Stir gently until well mixed and chilled.
Twist the orange peel over the drink to release its oils, then drop it into the glass.
Optionally, garnish with a cherry.

### Whiskey Sour
2 oz bourbon
3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
1/2 oz simple syrup
Ice
Lemon slice or cherry (for garnish)
Instructions:

Shake the bourbon, lemon juice, and simple syrup with ice.
Strain into a glass filled with ice.
Garnish with a lemon slice or cherry.


### Mojito
2 oz white rum
1/2 oz fresh lime juice
2 teaspoons sugar
6-8 fresh mint leaves
Soda water
Ice
Instructions:

Muddle the mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in a glass.
Fill the glass with ice, pour in the rum, and top with soda water.
Stir gently and garnish with a mint sprig.


### Gin & Tonic
2 oz gin
4 oz tonic water
Ice
Lime wedge
Instructions:

Fill a glass with ice.
Add gin and top with tonic water.
Squeeze a lime wedge into the drink and drop it in.

### Cosmopolitan
1 1/2 oz vodka
1 oz cranberry juice
1/2 oz triple sec
1/2 oz fresh lime juice
Ice
Lime or orange twist for garnish
Instructions:

Shake all ingredients with ice.
Strain into a chilled martini glass.
Garnish with a lime or orange twist.
These cocktails are easy to make at home, requiring just a few steps and ingredients. Enjoy!
